About me

I am Ali Mohamed Ali. I like doing things, and I like learning. I am open-minded so much so that I don't have favourites, except for my preferred text editor being GNU Emacs.

If you find me interesting, please feel free to send me an email. I am currently under the address <someguyshutup5@gmail.com>.

What is this website?

A personal hub for any of my writing to land on, and updates on any of my latest experiments. My opinions, my achievemnts, showcase of any of my knowledge. I believe that any knowledge must be shared for the benefit of all, and in the classic hacker adage `Information wants to be free.'

Why as a personal website?

I've chosen to do this as a website instead of a blog on a social networking site out of many reasons, but the most fruitful is that I am entirely capable of it. I am skilled enough to recreate any of the functions of a regular blogging platform, and so the real question would be why not?

Besides, this website isn't just a blog. It may contain extended texts that don't follow a blog format, and a personal website is — in my opinion — the only fitting place for something of this kind. The benefits of a unique domain name are considerable.

How is it managed

I write these from templates in GNU Emacs' Org mode. All site management is done through a collection of scripts in sh and elisp, so all the tenuous work of writing(in HTML), styling, and indexing pages is gone.

If I have a need for something dynamic, I would learn PHP, but as of now I couldn't be bothered.
